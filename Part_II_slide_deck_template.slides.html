Bike Service Hours for both users¶

Most Subscribers use the service at peak hours between 8 and 17 (5pm).

Customers have more flexible time, mostly used in the afternoon.

Bike service usage per day for both users¶

Subscribers tend to use the service during the week, probably for work related purposes while Customers have more flexible time.

Customers have a slight increase on thursday and friday but the rest of the day is relatively the same

Bike service usage per hour for both users¶

Subscribers seem to have a lot more stable use of the bikes than the Customers although Customer usage is also high.

Its interesting that Customers have a very high average duration around 3AM which is higher than every other hour
